About Me

Hello and thanks for checking out my portfolio! My name is Chris Mattix and I am currently a student in the UNC Full Stack Developer Bootcamp. I am a former teacher who spent 12 years in education and decided it was time to move on to a new career where I can put my problem-solving skills to the test.

I am originally from Montana, and miss my home state everyday. I have lived in North Carolina for the past 6 years, and truly love it...even if the mountains here don't have the same majesty as the ones in Montana.

I am an avid runner, home cook, coffee afficianado, and music lover. I am a proud resident of Durham, NC and am excited to enter the world of web development full-time once my coursework has concluded.
